New Hope in Gene Therapy: Trial Begins for Rare Disorder

March 12, 2025

In a groundbreaking medical step, scientists have begun testing a new form of gene therapy aimed at treating a rare genetic disorder that has long defied conventional treatments.

During the initial procedure, a 35-year-old man and a 50-year-old woman were treated with over 150 million modified immune cells. These cells carried a specially designed gene meant to repair faulty DNA responsible for the disease's progression.
