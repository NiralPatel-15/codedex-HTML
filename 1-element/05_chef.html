Vegetable Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, leftover rice)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • ½ cup green peas (frozen or fresh)
  • ½ capsicum/bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: chopped spring onions for garnish

Instruction:

  1. Prepare the rice: If you haven’t already, cook the rice and let it cool completely. Cold rice works best for fried rice.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok on medium heat.
  3. Sauté onion until soft and slightly golden.
  4. Add vegetables: Add chopped carrot, peas, and capsicum. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes until they’re tender but still slightly crisp.
  5. Add rice: Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the vegetables.
  6. Add seasoning: Pour in soy sauce, sprinkle salt and black pepper. Stir everything together for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Garnish & serve: Turn off the heat, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot!
